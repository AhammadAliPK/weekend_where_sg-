### Discovery Decisions
- DECISION: Color direction = Singapore tropical palette
- REASON: Balances Singapore identity (Garden City, orchid) with product needs (outdoor, fresh, calm, practical)
- COLORS LOCKED:
  - Primary: Tropical Green (fresh, outdoor, Garden City identity)
  - Secondary: Warm Wood/Earth (calming, grounded, heritage warmth)
  - Accent: Muted Orchid Purple (national flower, Singapore premium)
  - Neutral: Warm Gray/Sand (practical, Singapore efficiency, clean)

- DECISION: Target audience = Younger people (not aging population)
- REASON: Weekends, outdoor activity, mobile-first demographic
- IMPLICATION: Standard typography sizing, modern aesthetic, not over-optimized for accessibility

- DECISION: Form factor = Multi-surface (phone + tablet + desktop)
- REASON: Younger users use multiple devices, responsive design needed
- IMPLICATION: Mobile-first responsive design, tablet and desktop layouts considered

- DECISION: Component approach = Heavy customization of shadcn-svelte for Singapore identity
- REASON: Want authentic Singapore feel, not generic component library
- IMPLICATION: Customize button styles, card designs, badges, patterns for local identity

- DECISION: Design philosophy = Clean and minimal
- REASON: Fresh, practical, not over-designed
- IMPLICATIONS:
  - Typography: Inter (clean, modern, minimal)
  - Dark mode: Skip for MVP (keep simple)
  - Motion: Skip or very subtle (practical over flashy)
  - Patterns: Minimal, no heavy decorative elements

- DECISION: Stake level = Consumer Product
- REASON: Real Singapore users will interact with this, not just training demo
- IMPLICATION: Polished, user-facing, quality bar for all interactions and visual design

- DECISION: Working mode = Fast path
- REASON: User wants to progress quickly to review phase
- IMPLICATION: Draft both spines with [ASSUMPTION] tags, skip creative tools, iterate based on feedback
